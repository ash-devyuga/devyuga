FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Quick answers about how we work, AI, compliance, and what happens after launch.

Can't find your answer? Send us a message or book a call.

How do I get started with Devyuga's services?
  1. Book a free 30-minute discovery call (or send us a message below) to tell us about your idea, product, or problem.
  2. We dig into your requirements, constraints, and goals — and tell you honestly whether and how we can help.
  3. You receive a written proposal with scope options, timeline, and pricing tiers — no obligation, no pressure.
Most clients go from first call to kickoff within two weeks.
How long does it take to complete a project?
Most projects ship in 8–16 weeks, depending on complexity. We start with a short requirements phase (typically up to 4 weeks for larger systems), after which you get a detailed, realistic timeline with milestones. Because we build MVP-first, you see a working product early — usually within the first few sprints — rather than waiting until the end.
What is your project model and how is it priced?
We work in an Agile, MVP-first model: short sprints, regular demos, and your feedback folded in continuously. For every system we propose multiple tiers — for example, a lean MVP, a standard build, and a fully-featured version — so you choose the scope, timeline, and budget that fit. Pricing is agreed up front per tier; there are no surprise invoices. For ongoing work we also offer monthly retainer arrangements.
Can you add AI — including agentic AI — to my product?
Yes. We've shipped production systems with LLM integration and custom machine-learning models, and we design agentic AI pipelines — orchestrated LLM agents that handle multi-step workflows like triage, document processing, and research, with humans in the loop where it matters. Our approach is practical: we start from the business problem, not the technology, and we build in evaluation, guardrails, and fallbacks so AI features are reliable enough to trust — not just impressive in a demo.
Can you build HIPAA or PIPEDA compliant software?
Yes — this is our specialty. We design systems to support compliance with HIPAA (US), PIPEDA and provincial health acts like the Alberta HIA and Ontario PHIPA (Canada), and GDPR (EU): encryption in transit and at rest, role-based access control, audit logging, data residency, and BAA-compatible hosting architectures. Compliance ultimately rests with the organization that controls the data, so we work alongside your privacy officer or counsel and support privacy impact assessments. See our Security & Compliance page for the full picture.
Do you provide maintenance and support after launch?
Yes. Launch is the start, not the end. We offer ongoing maintenance and support plans covering monitoring and alerting, security patches and dependency updates, bug fixes, backups and recovery, and small enhancements — with response expectations agreed in writing. Prefer to take it in-house? We also do clean handovers: documented code, infrastructure runbooks, and training for your team.
Who owns the code and intellectual property?
You do. For custom development work, the code and IP we build for you belong to you on payment, as set out in the project agreement. Your code lives in repositories you can access from day one, and everything we ship is documented so any competent team can pick it up. We're also happy to sign NDAs before the first detailed conversation.
Can you work with our existing codebase or team?
Absolutely. Not every engagement is greenfield — we regularly audit, stabilize, and extend existing systems, and we can embed with your in-house developers as an extension of your team. We start with a code and infrastructure review so you get an honest assessment of what's solid, what's risky, and what we'd tackle first.
What industries do you specialize in?
Healthcare is where we've gone deepest — patient management, clinical workflows, rehabilitation tools, telehealth, and research software built around health-privacy requirements. Beyond healthcare, our work spans:
  • AI & LLM-powered platforms
  • MVP development for startups
  • Cloud & DevOps
  • Data engineering and analytics
  • Mobile apps (iOS & Android)
  • Fintech and consumer products
We work with everyone from solo founders to established organizations — see our work for examples.
