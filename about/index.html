Built in Calgary. Trusted in healthcare.

Devyuga Software Solutions Inc. is a software development company helping founders and growing businesses across North America turn ideas into secure, scalable products — with deep specialization in healthcare technology.

Our story

Why we exist

We started Devyuga because we saw too many good ideas fail in translation — between what founders envisioned and what got built. Great software needs both technical mastery and a genuine understanding of the business behind it.

Today we design and deliver web applications, mobile apps, cloud platforms, and AI-powered systems for clients ranging from solo founders to clinical organizations and university researchers. Healthcare is where we've gone deepest: clinic management platforms, rehabilitation tools, telehealth, and research software that handles sensitive data with the care it demands.

We're a product company as well as a development partner. Alongside client work, we build and operate our own SaaS products — including a multi-tenant clinic management platform — so every architecture decision we recommend is one we've lived with in production. Our next frontier is agentic AI pipelines: orchestrated LLM agents that take on real, multi-step business workflows.

We stay deliberately small and senior. When you work with Devyuga, the people in the discovery call are the people building your product.

How we work

Our approach

  • MVP-first, agile delivery. We start with a focused MVP, keep you involved in every sprint, and iterate based on real feedback — typical projects ship in 8–16 weeks.
  • Transparent scope and pricing. We propose multiple tiers for every system so you can choose the timeline and budget that fits.
  • Security and compliance by design. Privacy regulations like HIPAA and PIPEDA are design inputs, not afterthoughts. Read how we handle compliance.
  • Quality you can inherit. Rigorous testing, code review, and documentation — so your team can own the codebase long after launch.
What we stand for

Our values

Trust before transactions

We handle sensitive health data, client IP, and business-critical systems. Confidentiality and reliability aren't features — they're the baseline.

Outcomes over output

Lines of code don't matter; patient outcomes, conversion rates, and hours saved do. We measure our work by what it changes for your business.

Partnership, not vendorship

From brainstorming to launch and beyond, we work as an extension of your team — transparent communication, honest trade-offs, no surprises.
